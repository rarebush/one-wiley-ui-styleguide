--- notes: | 'Callouts' area a way to draw important information to the user with more subtlety than a full alert panel. They are available in the following flavours: - Success - Information - Warning - Danger ---

Everything is going great!

This type of callout should be used for positive information.

Business as usual

Please use this callout to notify the user of neutral information

Maybe the user should take note...

The information used in the style callout should be precautionary

Something will or has gone wrong!!!

Use this to notify users of an issue that will most likely affect their experience.
